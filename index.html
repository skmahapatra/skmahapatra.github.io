  • I am currently a doctoral research candidate at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I am associated with the Robotics and Design Lab, working under the guidance of Prof. Ashitava Ghosal, where my main focus of research is on continuum robots. My research interest also includes obstacle avoidance and path planning.

    A brief history

    I obtained my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata, in 2016. I completed my master's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Machine Design from Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad, in 2018. I worked on modelling and control of a twin rotor MIMO system using bondgraph modelling and Adams-MATLAB co-simulation as my masters thesis under the guidance of Prof. Sanjoy K. Ghoshal.
